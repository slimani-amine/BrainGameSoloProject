Welcome to Brain Games! Our collection features three engaging games: a guessing game, a question quiz, and an IQ challenge. Your scores in each game are weighted differently: the guessing game score is multiplied by 1, the question quiz score by 2, and the IQ challenge score by 3. To calculate your overall performance, we sum up the weighted scores and divide by 6.

Based on your global score, we provide an assessment: a score between 3 and 4 is very impressive, 2 to 3 is great, 1 to 2 is good, and below 1 is an opportunity for improvement.

Join us on this brain-boosting journey and unlock your mind's potential!
